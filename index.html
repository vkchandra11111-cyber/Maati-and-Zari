Our Name, Our Promise

Two words,
one thread

Some threads don't loosen with distance.

Living far from India shouldn't mean settling for less on the days that matter most — a wedding, a Diwali evening, your child's first festival abroad.

Maati and Zari exists to close that distance. We handpick ethnic wear from trusted weavers and boutiques across India, check every piece ourselves, and ship it to your doorstep, anywhere in the world.

No endless marketplaces. No guesswork. Just a conversation — you tell us the occasion, we find you the piece.
